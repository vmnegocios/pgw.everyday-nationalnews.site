Discover the Art of Sourdough Bread!
Your beginner's guide to handcrafted sourdough with minimal kneading.

Welcome to the world of artisan sourdough! With just flour, water, and salt, you can begin your journey to delicious homemade bread. "Artisan Sourdough Made Simple" is the ultimate guide, providing step-by-step instructions to help you create and care for your sourdough starter, plus more than 60 unique recipes.

Explore a Range of Flavors and Styles

From classic loaves to flavorful twists, this book features recipes like Roasted Garlic & Rosemary Bread, Multigrain Sandwich Bread, and No-Knead Tomato Basil Focaccia. Each recipe is designed for beginner bakers who want to experience the magic of sourdough without extensive kneading.

Healthier Bread, Naturally Fermented

Sourdough fermentation enhances digestion and is often easier on sensitive stomachs. Join the movement of homemade whole foods and enjoy a healthier, more flavorful bread experience.

Perfect for Every Occasion

Whether you're making a Sunday morning bagel or a raspberry twist for dessert, this book equips you with the knowledge to master sourdough baking at home, from your first rustic loaf to beyond.
